Albertan Oil Industry

Site Summary

For hundreds of years, Albertans have depended on the oil industry to fuel our economy. Our way of life has become very dependant on the non-renewable resource, and the dependancy has lit a controversy amoung the province. This site will help you explore the good, the bad, and the why, around Alberta's Oil Industry. Hopefully some insights can be gained, and you can read your way into an educated opinion on the topic at hand.
